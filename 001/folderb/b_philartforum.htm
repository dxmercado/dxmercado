SYNESTHESIA: Painting through the senses



"When he hears, he sees. When he listens, he catches glimpses.
And then he starts painting them. In coded secrets."









CALAMBA, Philippines – Visual artist and painter, Mr. Doel Salvador R. Mercado, conducted a Philippine Arts forum and exhibit yesterday (February 15) at the Media Center of Colegio de San Juan de Letran. This is under the L.A.F (Local Arts Forum) Out Loud project of fourth year AB Communication students in celebration of the National Arts Month.






Mr. Mercado is a member of the Art Association of the Philippines and the UP Painters’ Club. Together with a colleague, he spearheaded the creation of MakiSining, a social art group of fine artists practicing in Laguna. He served as MakiSining’s President in 2012.






He specializes in cryptographism, a visual arts style that highly incorporates mathematical codes and ciphers. He calls his pieces as coded art.






Know more about Mr. Mercado and his artworks at http://dsrmercado.wix.com/arts
